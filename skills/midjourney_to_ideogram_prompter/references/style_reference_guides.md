# Midjourney Art Style Reference Guides

This guide lists the precise visual tokens, structural requirements, technical settings, and visual prompt examples for each of the 12 clustered Midjourney art styles. Use these templates to build 50–200 line prompts.

---

## 1. chinese_ink_art (Traditional Chinese Ink Painting)
*   **Aesthetic Elements**: Flowing calligraphic brushwork, ink wash gradients, parchment textures, natural desaturated pigments, raked negative space.
*   **Technical Tokens**: `Song Dynasty folktale style`, `Chinese manuscript painting`, `delicate ink wash textures`, `parchment paper background`, `refined line art`, `museum-quality hand-painted scroll`.
*   **Recommended Settings**: High contrast black ink outlines combined with soft watercolor-like washes. Background should be designated as raw parchment or mulberry fiber paper.
*   **Prompt Example**: 
    > Ancient Chinese Empress Leizu seated peacefully beneath a mulberry tree, holding a porcelain tea cup with both hands, traditional flowing robes in muted ivory, champagne and sage tones, distant mountains and palace gardens behind her, calm contemplative expression, Song Dynasty inspired folklore illustration, traditional Chinese manuscript painting, delicate ink wash textures, elegant brushwork, parchment paper background, refined line art, timeless historical storytelling aesthetic, museum-quality hand-painted scroll artwork, no text of any kind on the image, vertical 9:16.

## 2. flat_nordic_illustration (Flat Nordic Folk Art & Storybook)
*   **Aesthetic Elements**: Minimalist shapes, flat color blocks (matte finish), continuous outlines without breaks, Scandinavian folk patterns, floral repeating designs.
*   **Technical Tokens**: `Scandinavian folk art aesthetic`, `Miffy meets Madeline`, `modern editorial illustration`, `flat color style`, `clean and slender continuous outlines`, `minimalist geometric aesthetic`, `cottagecore textile design`.
*   **Recommended Settings**: Flat 2D vector style. Solid backgrounds, no complex shadows, no depth gradients, crisp clean edges.
*   **Prompt Example**: 
    > A lime-slice-shaped Ferris wheel with sightseeing cabins sits on a level grassy area amidst the trees; features include small leaf-shaped cabins, layered green shrubbery, and a creative illustration of a forest in shades of grass green. The image employs a flat color style, clean and slender continuous outlines (with no breaks), and a minimalist geometric aesthetic; it boasts high-definition quality, crisp edges, and a clean, uncluttered composition.

## 3. impasto_oil_watercolor (Tactile Paint & Painterly Wash)
*   **Aesthetic Elements**: Thick paint ridges, textured impasto strokes, visible palette knife ridges, loose watercolor blends, paper bleeding, traditional canvas texture.
*   **Technical Tokens**: `impasto seascape oil painting`, `palette knife textures`, `visible brushstrokes`, `naive painting style`, `watercolor-like washes`, `wedding stationery style`, `distressed paper background`.
*   **Recommended Settings**: Explicitly describe paint thickness, canvas texture, and light reflecting off wet oil pigments or color bleeding on cold-pressed watercolor paper.
*   **Prompt Example**: 
    > This is an impressionistic, impasto seascape oil painting, employing distinct palette knife textures and skillfully capturing the fleeting light and shadow of dusk. The strong contrast between warm and cool colors--warm orange on the rocks, cool blue on the sea, and orange-red in the sky--creates rich layers of color, while the interplay of light and shadow creates a sense of depth and transparency. Sailboats often represent voyages, journeys, hope, and freedom; the sunset symbolizes fulfillment and gentle tranquility; and the coastal cliffs represent a sense of security and stability.

## 4. grotesque_comic_sketch (Grotesque Cartoon & Pulp Horror)
*   **Aesthetic Elements**: Absurd character anatomy, dramatic/shocked facial expressions, bold outlines, flat cartoon colors, distressed vintage printing texture, colored pencil shading.
*   **Technical Tokens**: `grotesque humor`, `cartoon illustration`, `Mike Judge meets MeatCanyon`, `absurd character design`, `1950's pulp horror`, `retro distressed comic book style`, `colored pencil line drawing`.
*   **Recommended Settings**: Specifying hand-drawn sketches, non-realistic proportions, bold black outlines, paper texture, and simple two-tone colors.
*   **Prompt Example**: 
    > A white cat on the brunette female head, cartoon illustration, grotesque humor, clean vector lines, bold colors, Mike Judge meets MeatCanyon, highly detailed, absurd character design.

*   **Aesthetic Elements**: Glitching particles, dissolving boundaries, micro-glitter dispersion, Aurora sky effects, dreamlike volumetric lighting, double exposure silhouettes.
*   **Technical Tokens**: `surreal SimAIty-style`, ` seeds dissolving into glittering particles`, `glitch fragments`, `mother-of-pearl reflections`, `opal-like iridescence`, `particle dispersion`, `soft edge erosion effect`, `magical realism`.
*   **Recommended Settings**: Luminous highlights, soft focus bokeh, dark moody negative space backgrounds to make iridescent particles pop.
*   **Prompt Example**: 
    > beautiful woman side profile, long flowing hair, elegant silhouette, semi-realistic CG portrait, hair strands moving in the wind, the edge of her silhouette gradually dissolving into ultra-fine iridescent glitter particles, pearlescent shimmer dust, holographic micro glitter, mother-of-pearl reflections, opal-like iridescence, tiny reflective particles floating away, subtle particle dispersion, soft edge erosion effect, dark minimalist background, black and deep navy color palette, silver-blue-violet iridescent highlights, high contrast silhouette, cinematic negative space, ethereal but restrained, luxury editorial aesthetic, dreamlike atmosphere, visual focus on silhouette transformation, clean composition, not galaxy, not nebula, not smoke, not liquid, not crystal shards, masterpiece 1:1.

## 8. cinematic_photography (Cinematic & Fashion Editorial Realism)
*   **Aesthetic Elements**: Photorealistic skin textures, depth of field, volumetric light shafts, dramatic camera perspectives, editorial high-fashion campaign styling.
*   **Technical Tokens**: `ultra realistic cinematic fashion portrait`, `ARRI Alexa 65`, `Vogue editorial`, `Hasselblad medium format`, `atmospheric depth`, `volumetric shaft of light`, `luxury land magazine photography`.
*   **Recommended Settings**: Specific camera lens naming (e.g. 24mm, 85mm), lighting sources (e.g. golden hour sunlight, single rectangular skylight), camera heights (e.g. extreme low angle ground perspective), and skin pores / fabric weave specifications.
*   **Prompt Example**: 
    > Ultra realistic cinematic fashion portrait of a beautiful Armenian woman in ancient temple ruins. Extreme low angle ground perspective, camera almost at floor level pointing upward, dramatic 24mm wide angle lens, making her appear powerful and larger than life with monumental stone columns towering above. She sits elevated on ancient stone ruins, centered between massive weathered classical columns. Voluminous handwoven ivory Armenian textile dress cascades outward in large sculptural folds, filling the foreground. Regal pose: upright torso, open shoulders, arms extended gracefully, head slightly tilted downward toward camera, embodying an ancient Armenian queen. Distinct Armenian beauty: olive skin, high cheekbones, almond shaped dark brown eyes, elegant nose, photorealistic skin texture, long dark braided hair. Elaborate traditional Armenian gold headpiece with intricate metalwork and cascading chains draped over forehead, nose, and mouth. Warm golden hour sunlight, atmospheric haze, dust particles, cinematic shadows. Ultra detailed fabric and stone textures, ARRI Alexa 65, HDR, cinematic color grading, Vogue editorial, cultural heritage fashion campaign, hyperrealism, masterpiece quality.

## 10. direct_flash_dusk (Grainy CCD & Raw Flash Photography)
*   **Aesthetic Elements**: Saturated greens, grainy digital noise (CCD sensor style), bloom and halation, lens flare stars, on-camera flash shadows, ordinary landscapes made surreal.
*   **Technical Tokens**: `direct camera flash at dusk/night`, `grainy CCD sensor noise`, `bloom and halation`, `lens flare stars`, `ordinary landscapes made uncanny`.
*   **Recommended Settings**: Explicitly block cinematic blockbuster lighting, epic compositions, or fantasy creatures. Specify direct on-camera flash.
*   **Prompt Example**: 
    > direct camera flash at dusk/night * deep blue and violet skies * saturated greens * grainy CCD sensor noise * bloom and halation * lens flare stars * slightly blurred focus * low-resolution digital camera * suburban ponds, meadows, forests, flowers * ordinary landscapes made uncanny * no epic compositions * no fantasy creatures * no cinematic blockbuster lighting.

## 12. 3d_pixar_unreal (Stylized 3D Character Rendering)
*   **Aesthetic Elements**: Cozy warm lighting, highly expressive clay-like skin models, depth of field, blurry background details, Octane rendering textures.
*   **Technical Tokens**: `3D Pixar animation style`, `relatable character`, `octane render`, `unreal engine 5`, `cinematic lighting`, `highly detailed textures`.
*   **Recommended Settings**: Stylized character anatomy (large expressive eyes), 85mm lens simulations, soft bokeh.
*   **Prompt Example**: 
    > 3D Pixar animation style, highly detailed and expressive. A cute, relatable 62-year-old grandmother sitting comfortably in a cozy armchair in a warm, sunlit living room. She is holding a ceramic coffee mug in one hand and gently touching her knee with the other hand, showing a slight expression of discomfort. Morning sunlight streaming through a nearby window, casting soft, warm, golden hour lighting. A blurry orthopedic pillow and a soft blanket in the background. Close-up shot, 85mm lens, depth of field, soft bokeh, cinematic lighting, cozy morning atmosphere, highly detailed textures, octane render, unreal engine 5.
